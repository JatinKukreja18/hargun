Product Description

Marine ply can withstand severe climatic conditions. In order to maintain good quality standard, quality veneers of uniform thickness are bonded with BWP grade of P.F.resin conforming to IS 848:1974. Finally it is treated in a vacuum pressure impregnation plant, using specified quality of preservatives to render it resistant to marine organisms. It can be widely used in bus and truck bodies, railway coaches, kitchen accessories, sign boards, paneling, partitions, bathrooms.
Marine Plywood has to be checked thoroughly for getting the assures quality. Although, routine vigilance of quality is done at every step but the team makes sure that every piece should undergo double checking by technically trained professionals. Marine ply can withstand rigorous climatic conditions, alternate dry and wet weather of coastal areas. This marine ply is dimensionally stable and ideal for long lasting services even in very humid and sea side areas. It can resist boiling water up to unexpected extent
