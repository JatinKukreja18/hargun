Our Story

Driven by the motive to achieve exuberant success in the Plywood Industry, we, Hargun Industries, are committed to provide world class Plywood to our large customer base. Established in the year 1994, we have become one of the most trusted and reliable names in the domain of plywood manufacturing. With over one decade of industry experience and expertise, we have marked our strong prominence in the domestic & International market. Today, we are regarded as a leading manufacturer, supplier & exporter in this competitive market. We are supplying a wide spectrum of ply under the brand names of "RIMPLY" and "FERRO".

Quality has been the prime motive of our company, since inception. To ascertain optimum quality of our products, we undertake stringent measures before, during as well as after the manufacturing process. In our quality control process, our in-house well equipped state of the art laboratory and a team of experienced quality controllers assist us in maintaining highest industrial standards for all our products. They make sure that all the processes are carried out with utmost precision and care.

Moreover, to ensure zero defects, the controllers check the entire range of finished products on varied parameters like Tensile strength, termite resistance, boiling water test, weather resistance, etc. The product range offered by us is ISI marked which in itself speaks for the quality standards of our plywood products.

We, at Hargun Industries, are empowered with a robustly constructed infrastructure incorporating advanced manufacturing, designing, quality checking, packaging, and other facilities. Built over a large area, our sophisticated manufacturing facility is outfitted with modern machinery. Various machines that assist us in producing a durable array of plywood include peeling machines, dryers, hot-presses, glue manufacturing kettle machines, plywood testing machines, drilling machines, polishing machines, wood seasoning chambers, and other technical purpose machines.

To develop as well as retain a competitive edge in manufacturing Marine Plywood, Commercial Plywood, MR Grade Plywood, Film Face Shuttering Plywood, Block Board, and Flush Door, we strive to upgrade our facilities in accordance with the latest market developments. Furthermore, we also lay strong emphasis on continuous research and development in order to develop new products.
